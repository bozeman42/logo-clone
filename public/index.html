You can click on the drawing space to place the turtle wherever you wish.

Commands

fd 20
Move the turtle forward 20 pixels
bk 20
Move the turtle backward 20 pixels
rt 20
Rotate the turtle 20° clockwise
lt 20
Rotate the turtle 20° counter-clockwise
pu
Pen up: allows you to move the turtle without drawing its path
pd
Pen down: the turtle will draw its path as it moves via the other commands
repeat 4 [ fd 100 rt 90 ]
repeat the commands inside the brackets. Spaces are required around the brackets.
[colorname] e.g. black or deepskyblue
change the pen color to a named web color of your choice
